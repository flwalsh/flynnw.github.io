Biscotti

Ingredients

  • 140g almonds, toasted, coarsely chopped (1 c.)

Small bowl

  • 250g flour (1 3/4 c.)
  • 8g baking powder (2 tsp.)
  • 2g salt (1/4 tsp.)
  • 35g almonds, toasted, finely chopped (1/4 c.)

Large bowl

  1. 2 eggs, whisked
  2. 200g sugar
  3. 56g butter, melted, cooled (1/2 stick)
  4. 6g almond extract (1 1/2 tsp.)
  5. 2g vanilla extract (1/2 tsp.)

Procedure

  1. Add dry to wet, mix to combine.
  2. Stir in 140g coarsely chopped almonds
  3. Baked 25 min (golden)
  4. Cool 30 min
  5. Slice 1/2" thick
  6. Bake 35 mins (golden brown)
