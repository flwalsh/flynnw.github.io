My Sourdough Recipe

This 75% hydration, 15% whole wheat loaf draws from a mix of this King Arthur recipe and Ken Forkish’s Flour Water Salt Yeast.

Ingredients:

  1. 300g 100% hydration starter (e.g.)
  2. 700g all purpose flour (I use King Arthur flours)
  3. 150g whole wheat flour
  4. 600g room temperature water
  5. 22g salt

Procedure:

  1. Feed starter 4-6 hours before mixing—if starter is kept in a mason jar, it should be just about overflowing.
  2. Mix ingredients and knead until just smooth, waiting at least 10 minutes between folds (usually 2-3).
  3. Rise overnight (~8-10 hours), until roughly tripled in volume.
  4. Halve, shape, and let proof until fluffy and lumpless, no more than an hour.
  5. Bake covered for 30 mins at 475 F, then until desired doneness, about 15 mins (I like golden to dark brown).
