My sourdough recipe

Last modified 2019-04-16

This 75% hydration 15% whole wheat loaf draws from a mix of this King Arthur recipe and Ken Forkish’s Flour Water Salt Yeast.

Ingredients:

  1. 500g 100% hydration starter (e.g.)
  2. 600g all purpose flour (I use King Arthur flours)
  3. 150g whole wheat flour
  4. 500g room temperature water
  5. 22g salt

Procedure:

  1. Feed starter 4-8 hours before mixing—if starter is kept in a mason jar, it should be just about overflowing.
  2. Mix ingredients and let autolyse for 20-30 mins (optional).
  3. Fold 4 times, until smooth.
  4. Rise 8-10 hours, until roughly tripled in volume.
  5. Halve, shape, and let proof until fluffy and lumpless, 1-1.25 hours.
  6. Bake covered for 25 mins at 475 F, then until desired doneness, 5-10 mins (I like golden to dark brown).
