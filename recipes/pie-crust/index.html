Pie Crust

Ingredients

  • 300g all-purpose flour
  • 6g salt (1 tsp.)
  • 227g cold butter (2 sticks)
  • 60g+ ice water

Procedure

  1. Mix 300g flour with 6g salt.
  2. Work in butter until distributed.
  3. Add ice water until mixture comes together.
  4. Refrigerate for 30 mins. before rolling.
