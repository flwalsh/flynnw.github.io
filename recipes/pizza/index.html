Pizza Dough

Last modified 2019-04-17

Ingredients (per ~12” pizza):

  1. 75g all purpose flour
  2. 75g 00 pizza flour (all purpose can be substituted)
  3. 5g salt
  4. 100g tap water
  5. 2g olive oil
  6. 1g yeast

Procedure:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl, working until thoroughly combined.
  2. Let rest 15 minutes, then knead another 2 minutes.
  3. Rise 2-3 hours, divide if necessary, and shape.

Dough lasts in the fridge for a day and weeks if frozen.
