Quick pizza dough

Last modified 2019-04-17

Inspired somewhat by this New York Times recipe, this is my go-to recipe for when I want pizza in under an hour. Roberta’s is still my favorite dough overall if you have an afternoon.

Ingredients (per ~12” pizza):

  1. 200g 00 pizza flour (all purpose can be substituted)
  2. 125g warm water
  3. 25g oliveoil
  4. 5g salt
  5. 2g yeast

Procedure:

  1. Combine ingredients and knead thoroughly, until almost smooth, about 5 minutes.
  2. Let rest a few minutes, then knead until totally smooth.
  3. Rise ~20 minutes, divide if necessary, and flour lightly, and shape.

Dough lasts in the fridge for a day and weeks if frozen.
