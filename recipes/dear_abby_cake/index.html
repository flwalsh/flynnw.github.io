Dear Abby Cake

Cake

Makes two moist and chocolatey 8” cake layers.

Ingredients

  • 3 oz. unsweetened chocolate (total 4 oz.)
  • 1⁄2 cup butter (total 9 tbsp.)
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 cups cake flour (sifted)
  • 1.25 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 eggs (total 5)
  • 1 cup (8 oz.) sour cream
  • 2 cups sugar (total 3 cups)
  • 1.5 tsp. vanilla (total 3 tsp.)

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
  2. Melt 3 oz. chocolate with 1⁄2 cup butter and 1 cup water in top of double boiler. Let cool.
  3. Butter and lightly flour two 8” round cake pans (even if non-stick).
  4. Sift 2 cups cake flour, 1.25 tsp baking soda, and 1 tsp salt into a large bowl.
  5. Beat eggs with sour cream in a medium bowl. Beat in sugar and vanilla. Stir into cooled chocolate mixture.
  6. Beat into flour mixture, half at a time, just until smooth. Batter will be thin. Pour evenly into prepared pans.
  7. Bake at 350 F for 40 minutes or until center springs back at press at the touch.
  8. Let pans cool on racks. Loosen cakes around edges and turn out onto racks.
  9. Make frosting (see below).
  10. Place one cake onto serving platter or easily transportable base. Join cakes with a quarter of the frosting and cover sides and top with remaining, swirling with a spatula.
  11. Drizzle with melted chocolate garnish (see below).

Frosting

Ingredients:

  • 3 egg whites
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3⁄4 tsp cream of tartar
  • dash of salt
  • 2.5 tsp. water
  • 1.5 tsp. vanilla

Procedure:

  1. Beat all but vanilla in a double boiler over simmering water until it forms firm peaks, about 7 minutes.
  2. Remove from boiler and stir in vanilla.

Garnish

  • 1 oz. unsweetened chocolate
  • 1 tbsp. butter
  1. Mix in microwave or cup in double boiler; stir until smooth.
